Arena of Talents

The Arena of Talents (AoT) is one of the four types of PvP Arenas. In this Arena, random 3vs3 teams can match for combat. It has its own rules and schedules.
  • To access the Arena of Talents you need to be at least level 99(+1).
  • You can only participate five times per day.

Schedules​

Arena of Talents will open at 11:00 and 19:00 (Server Time) on Channel 4 and will stay open for an hour before closing.

How to participate in AoT​

To access the map of this arena, first you have to access the Arena of Glory map, then you will have to talk to the NPC Tei Ken located at the center of the arena. There, you will find two options to join the Arena:

  • Registration: With this option, you can register as a member of a team. A timer will appear at the bottom right of the screen during the matchmaking process. Once the system finds a match, you will be teleported into the arena. Inside, you will have 60 seconds to get organized before starting the fights.

  • As a spectator: If you want to see how the other players play, you can join as a spectator, to switch between players' perspectives, you can simply use the camera icon.

Rewards Shop​

When you win an AoT you will receive a number of Gold Medals, which you can redeem in the Arena Shop. Arena Shop has the following items:

  • Titan Wings = x150 Gold Medal

    • Damage dealt in PvP is increased by 10%.
    • Damage received in PvP is reduced by 5%.
    • Movement speed is increased by 1.

  • 'Joey' the Kangaroo= x200 Gold Medal

    • Movement speed: 22
    • With Speed Booster, removes all bad effects up level 4, movement speed +3.

  • Duelist: = x100 Gold Medal

    • Attack power is increased by 10% during an attack in the team arena.
    • Damage taken in the arena is reduced by 10%.

  • Mysterious Box of Completion: = x10 Gold Medal

  • Arena of Masters Ticket:= x2 Gold Medal

  • Rune Chest: = x25 Mithril Medal*

*Mithril Medal can be obtained through many PvP related achievements.
