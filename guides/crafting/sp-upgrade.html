Perfectioning the SP​

Malcolm Mix and several other NPC's offer you the ability to perfect specialist cards. This is in addition of the SP card upgrades. Every perfection upgrade gives an extra bonus. The maximum amount of perfection is 100. The highest amount of points in one stat is 55.

Bonus abilities​

There are 8 different categories in which you can gain bonus abilities. Every level of perfection increases the basic stats of the character wearing the SP card.

  • Attack (+10 attack)
  • Defense (+10 defense)
  • Element (+1 element)
  • HP/MP (+100 HP/MP)
  • Fire resistance (+1 resistance)
  • Water resistance (+1 resistance)
  • Light resistance (+1 resistance)
  • Shadow resistance (+1 resistance)

The SP card below has the following stats which translates into:

Attack: 30 (+300 attack)
Defense: 47 (+470 defense)
Element: 49 (+49 element)
HP/MP: 33 (+3300 HP/MP)
Fire res.: 8 (+8% fire resistance)
Water res.: 6 (+6% water resistance)
Light res.: 2 (+2% light resistance)
Shadow res.: 12 (+12% shadow resistance)

Upgrade menu​

Olympus has their own unique way of perfecting the SP card. In the upgrade menu you can choose how many tries you want to gamble on the SP card. You can start the upgrading. The chat will show you the results of the upgrading.

The available options for tries are: 1, 5, 10, 15 and 20. The upgrade cost will be increased or decreased, depending on selected tries. The upgrading will automatically stop at a multiple of 10, such as 40 or 50. This means if you have 48 perfection points in the SP card and you select 20 tries, it will try upgrading it to 50 for 20 tries. If it is succeeded on the 3rd try, it won't attempt the other 17 tries.

The upgrading menu with 10 tries selected​

When upgrading, the results are shown in chat. It is possible to fail all tries at once. The higher perfection the SP card gets, the more chance of failing perfecting the SP card.

The menu view with the upgraded card​

The perfection stats are shown at the bottom of the menu.
