Witch Laurena Raid - Guide

How can I get the seals?​

  • Port Alveus Square with the NPC Lilith

Recommendations for the Raids​

  • Use a Light SP with 100 element.

Raid Mechanics​

Laurena has a variety of spells​

  • Thorn Bush: Movement impossible,Every 4 seconds Hp is reduced by 1000 and no attack possible - A nearby player must eliminate the debuff

  • Laurena's dark magic transform the target into a bushi: The player must go to the bushes to undo the spell

  • Laurena's dark magic transform the target into a Hamster: The player must go to the cheeses to undo the spell

When Laurena loses a certain amount of HP, the Laurena's Beast will appear.

Raid Map

Bonus​

  • Complete these raid 5 times (daily) and 25 times (weekly) will reward you with x20 Mystic Gemstone and 5M
