Act 7 Questline guide

Guide for the Act 7.1 Main Questline with Mobs, NPC Spots and Quest items.
To start the Act 7.1 Main Questline you have to be lv. 99+30.
If you start with lvl 99+50 you will end up in the end with lvl 99+55

You can get a7 questline from Merchant Ben at Port Alveus Square

Couse for the Arrival ​

Talk to Propga, leader of the orcs in Mortaz Desert Harbour.
Conversation with [Propga]

Off to New Shores! ​

Talk to Propga in Mortaz Desert Harbour to travel to the orc island.
Move to the designated location on the map: Port Moritius


Quest Reward: CXP

The Harbour Guard ​

Talk to Perkichan in Port Moritius to find out what happened.
Conversation with [Perkichan]

Perkichan's Worries ​

Perkichan seems to want to tell you something. Talk to him again at Port Moritius.
Conversation with [Perkichan]

Defeat the monsters on the beach so the fishermen can go about their work in peace.
Hunt 20 [Merling]
Hunt 10 [Siren]

They can be found in Port Moritius and South Beach
