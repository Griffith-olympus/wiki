Act 6 Questline guide

Guide for the Act 6 Questline with Mobs, NPC Spots and Quest items. To start the Act 6 Questline you have to be lv. 99. Estimated time for the whole Questline approx. 45 Minutes - 1 Hour

  1. Forgotten Archipelago 2
  • Conversation with Crusader Graham
  1. Forgotten Archipelago 3
