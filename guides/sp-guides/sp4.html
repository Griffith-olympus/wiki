You can find the Grey Soulstone on the marked place in Fir Forest

SP4 - Questlineâ€‹

Like the other SP Questlines, the Time-Spaces are gone but unlike the first two SP Questlines are not replaced by killing monsters. So it is just shortened and also some of the needed monster kill counts were reduced. Another change is, that everything has a 100% droprate. In case of accidentally deleting a quest item, you can buy them again from the treasure box next to the Soulstone for 1kk.

  1. Talk with Soty Bystander (Watermill).
  2. Hunt Hunchbacked Zombie Guard and collect 1x Whitney's Treatise.

You can access the Secret lab through Eastern Krem, then going into the Misty Path.

  1. Hunt Skull Warrior and collect 1x Natalie's Headband.

  1. Talk with Soty Bystander (Watermill).
  2. Talk to Grey Soulstone. Instead of a TS you get to talk to the Grey Soulstone again.
  3. Talk to the Grey Soulstone.
  4. Talk with Eri Windspear (Warehouse).
  5. Hunt 40 Yak Warrior - receive Piepe's Necklace.

  1. Hunt 30 Strong Tree Zombies - receive Wooden Wolf Doll.

  1. Talk to Eri Windspear (Warehouse).
  2. Talk to Grey Soulstone.
  3. Talk to Grey Soulstone.
  4. Talk with Rect Lat (Maple Woods).
  5. Hunt Wollet and collect 1x Seina's Ring (Maple Woods).

  1. Hunt Greylander and collect 1x Demon Crystal (Maple Woods' edge).

  1. Talk to Rect Lat (Maple Woods).
  2. Talk to Grey Soulstone.
  3. Talk to Grey Soulstone.
  4. Talk to Rody Windsinger (Forester's House).
  5. Talk to Soty Bystander (Watermill).
  6. Pay 3.000.000 gold to Soty Bystander (Watermill).
  7. Hunt King Leech and collect 1x Unknown Ore.

  1. Hunt Mole Cricket and collect 1x Seli-Lusha leaf (Forest floor - eastern).

  1. Hunt 20 Mole Mother (Underground Path <- Maple Woods' Edge).

  1. Hunt 20 Large Jellyfish.

  1. Hunt 10 Sentinel I.

  1. Talk to Soty Bystander (Watermill).

Now you gotta craft the spear with the recipe, using the ingredients you got from killing the monsters. If you had no space in your inventory, they should be in your mail.

  1. Craft the Spear and talk to Eri Windspear (Warehouse).
  2. Talk to Soty Bystander (Watermill).
  3. Talk to Grey Soulstone.
  4. Talk to Grey Soulstone.

Congratulations, you received your 4th Specialist!.
