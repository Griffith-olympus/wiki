Talk with the SP3 Soulstone on the marked place in Peak of Mt. Krem.

SP3 - Questlineâ€‹

Like the SP1 and SP2 Questlines, their Time-Spaces are gone but unlike the other two SP Questlines are not replaced by killing monsters. So it is just shortened and also some of the needed item amounts were reduced. Another change is, that everything has a 100% droprate.

In case of accidentally deleting a quest item, you can buy them again from the treasure box next to the Soulstone for 1kk each as seen below:

  1. Talk with Soraya Style.
  2. Collect 50 Bloody Stone from Weak Skeleton Warrior (100%).

  1. Talk with Soraya Style.
  2. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  3. Talk with Calvin Coach.
  4. Collect Torn Page from Old Dust Death (100%).

  1. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  2. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  3. Talk with Eva Energy.
  4. Collect 40 Bean Pod from Quick Thorn Pii (100%).

  1. Collect 30 Flint from Lava Golem (100%).

  1. Collect 30 Straw from Wiggler (100%).

  1. Collect 30 Special Water Bottle from Rock Turtle (100%).

  1. Collect Special Bleach Recipe from Foggy Colossus.

  1. Craft Special Bleach with Special Bleach Recipe and Talk to Eva Energy.
  2. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  3. Talk with Anni.
  4. Pay Anni 1kk Gold.
  5. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  6. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  7. Talk with Knight Pinto.
  8. Collect Holy Water Bottle from Holy Well.

  1. Talk with Knight Pinto.
  2. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  3. Talk with Commander Ray Ramos.
  4. Collect Triangular Key from Weak Deathwitch.

  1. Collect Small Safe from Iron Torturer.

  1. Talk with Commander Ray Ramos.
  2. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  3. Talk with Golden Soulstone.
  4. Talk with Golden Soulstone.

Congratulations, you received your 3rd Specialist.
