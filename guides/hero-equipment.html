C-Level 25 - 28 equipment

Raidboxes​

Crafting​

By using 'Ancelloan's Production Scrolls' you can convert your level 95 equipment to C-Level 25-28 equipment.

  • Heavenly equipment -> 60x Holy Blood and 50x Crystal of Balance.
  • Hellord equipment -> 60x Dark Energy Stone and 50x Crystal of Balance.

Ancelloan's Weapon Production Scroll

Ancelloan's Secondary Weapon Production Scroll

Ancelloan's Armour Production Scroll

Where to farm materials​

  • Holy Blood -> Heaven's Side (drops from mobs)
  • Dark Energy Stone -> Hell's Side (drops from mobs)
  • Crystal of Balance -> Craft at 'Priest of Transformation' by using Essence of Balance (Obtainable from raids)

You can trade your Hellord Supply Ticket and Mystic Heaven Supply Ticket at 'Crusader Graham' in exchange for raid seals, equipment scrolls and C-Level 30 helmets. Hellord and Heaven Supply Tickets are obtained by completing Daily Quests.
