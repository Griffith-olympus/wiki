# Arena of Talents
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://imageshack.com/i/pmALyIT0j" /></p>
    The Arena of Talents (AoT) is one of the four types of PvP Arenas. In this Arena, random 3vs3 teams can match for combat. It has its own rules and schedules.

- To access the Arena of Talents you need to be at least level 99(+1).
- You can only participate five times per day.

## Schedules

Arena of Talents will open at 11:00 and 19:00 (Standart Time) on Channel 4 and will stay open for an hour before closing.

## How to participate in AoT
To access the map of this arena, first you have to access the Arena of Glory map, then you will have to talk to the NPC Tei Ken located at the center of the arena. There, you will find two options to join the Arena:
- **Registration:** With this option, you can register as a member of a team. A timer will appear at the bottom right of the screen during the matchmaking process. Once the system finds a match, you will be teleported into the arena. Inside, you will have 60 seconds to get organized before starting the fights.
<p align="center">
  <img src="https://imageshack.com/i/pnnr6Irqp"/></p>
- **As a spectator:** If you want to see how the other players play, you can join as a spectator, to switch between players' perspectives, you can simply use the camera icon. ![](https://imageshack.com/i/pndk519cj)
