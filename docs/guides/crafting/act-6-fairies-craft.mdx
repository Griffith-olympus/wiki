# Act 6 Fairy Craft - Guide
Act6 have three generations of fairies: Zenas, Erenia, Fernon. They have their effects and crafting recipies modified to make it less irritating and frustrating. RNG factor of their craft has been removed, meaning that gathering the necessary materials you will get the fairy with certainity. Also, fairies start at level 80 and do not level up.

## Changes in the appearance of fairies.
Visual appearance of the fairies have been changed according to their element, this will help to identify them more easily.

## How to Craft
To craft the Zenas, Erenia and Fernon Fairies, you should head to Cylloan, once there, you will have to go to the west of the map and talk to **Priest of transformation**, who has the recipes for crafting these fairies, and more.

<p align="center">

To craft the Zenas and Erenia Fairies you will need 6 different items, some of which will vary depending on the element and the type of fairy (Zenas/Erenia).
<p align="center">
<img src="https://imageshack.com/i/pmT06Aztp"/></p>

To craft a Fernon Fairy, you will need one Erenia and one Zenas fairy of the same element as the Fernon fairy you want to craft, along with some more items.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://imageshack.com/i/po7CJdPzp"/></p>

- **Fernon's Egg:**: Awarded as a random prize inside the Raid Boxes: Fernon the Destroyer <ItemIcon iconId="2838"/> .
- **Fernon's Energy:** We can obtain this item by farming on the Act 6.2 maps (tradeable/sellable) <ItemIcon iconId="2859"/>.
- **Fairy King's Essence:** We can craft this item using the four Divine Fairies.<ItemIcon iconId="2850"/>
- **Divine Fairy**: Depending on the element we want, we need a divine fairy of that element, be it <ItemIcon iconId="988"/> (<font color="cyan">Water</font>), <ItemIcon iconId="987"/> (<font color="red">Fire</font>), <ItemIcon iconId="989"/> (<font color="gold">Light</font>) or <ItemIcon iconId="993"/> (<font color="indigo">Shadow</font>).

	These can be obtained through the Olympus Shop (OC) in random boxes or they can be acquired as prizes in the family dungeons of Glacernon.

- **Erenia's/ZenaS'/Fernon's Egg**: Depending on the type of fairy we want to craft we will need one of the two eggs. These eggs can be obtained from the respective raid boxes as a rare loot.

- **Holy Water of the Order**: This item <ItemIcon iconId="1154"/> can be purchased at the Supply Center from the NPC **Priest of Supply** in Cylloan for a price of 100kk.

- **Essences**: You will need essence of <font color="indigo"> Varik </font><ItemIcon iconId="2849"/> ,<font color="cyan">  Ladine </font><ItemIcon iconId="2847"/> , <font color="gold"> Rumial </font><ItemIcon iconId="2848"/>  or <font color="red">  Elkaim </font><ItemIcon iconId="2846"/>  (depending on the element we want the fairy).
You can get the essences from the family dungeons of Glacernon, or from the military supplies random box that you can get from Graham in exchange for Hellord and Mystic Heaven tickets.

- **Essences**: You will need essence of <font color="red">  Sellaim </font><ItemIcon iconId="2842"/> , <font color="indigo"> Turik </font><ItemIcon iconId="2845"/> ,  <font color="gold">Eperial </font><ItemIcon iconId="2844"/>  or <font color="cyan">Woondine </font><ItemIcon iconId="2843"/> . 
You can get the essences from the family dungeons of Glacernon, or from the military supplies random box that you can get from Graham in exchange for Hellord and Mystic Heaven tickets.

- **Crystal of Balance** <ItemIcon iconId="2755"/>: You can craft them from Element of Balance which in turn becomes  Essence of Balance in the NPC **Priest of Transformation**.
The element, the essence and the crystal of balance can be obtained as a random prize in the Zenas, Erenia and Fernon raid boxes. The Element of Balance can also be obtained by farming in Act6.1 and Act6.2 maps.
