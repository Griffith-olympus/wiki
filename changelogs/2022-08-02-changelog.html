Changelog - 02/08/2022

August 2, 2022 · 3 min read
Jundi
Wiki Manager

02/08/2022 - 10:30 (8:30 ST)
Server Version: 1.356.0
Client Version: 1.134.0

Features​

  • Added "Boxes Pity" Tab in box content window
  • Wings Swapper
  • Raid Report Window
  • Bidding System
  • Public Warps
  • "Receive All" button for mails
  • Bazaar search QoL (drag & drop item in search bar)

Raids QoL (v1)​

  • The seal will now be consumed when entering the raid and not on team creation
  • The team won't be destroyed after the raid

Changes​

  • Added Recipe for the Unfinished Priest's Spear to Sp4 Quest Shop
  • Added a new general achievement that gives an infinite use Cuarry Bank Debit Card.
  • Added "There is a 100% chance that every attack hits." to Perti and Chloe's debuffer skills.
  • Added unique icons to each wings' buff.
  • Reverted arena changes:
    • Removed spectator buff and brought back safe zone
    • Enabled mates' attack
    • Enabled potion use

Fixes​

  • Fixed skills visually disappearing under certain circumstances
  • Fixed valhalla partners skill cooldown on partner level up
  • Fixed a bug where you wouldn't be able to receive any buffs with certain reflection buffs
  • Fixed a bug where "Fernon Punishment" debuff would be applied twice instead of once
  • Fixed "Another Meeting" Valhalla quest pointing wrong direction for Ragnar's location.

SP & Items Balance​

Fire Cannoneer ​

  • [Great Explosive Shockwave]
    • Duration: 3s -> 4s
  • [Explosive Enhancement]
    • [NEW] Ranged attack is increased by (Player Level x2)
  • [Turbo Loader]
    • [REMOVE] Ranged attack is increased by (Player Level x2)

Scout ​

  • Lightning Reload:
    • Cool time: 15s -> 18s
  • Mobile Trap:
    • Cool time: 18s -> 25s

Berserker ​

  • [Berserker]:
    • Movement speed is increased by 4 -> 3
  • Sonic Rush:
    • Cool time: 7s -> 10s

Gladiator ​

  • Taunt:
    • Cool time: 40s -> 60s
  • Engorgement:
    • [REMOVE] Restores (Player Level x30) HP

Volcano ​

  • [Magma Shield]:
    • [NEW] Movement speed is increased by 1
  • [Lava Swamp]:
    • [REMOVE] The water element is nullified
    • [NEW] Non-elemental damage received is increased by 15%
  • [Poisonous Volcanic Gas]:
    • [NEW] Non-elemental damage received is increased by 5%

Tide Lord ​

  • Typhoon:
    • There is a 30% -> 50% chance of causing [Small Waterfall]
  • The Large Trident:
    • There is a 20% -> 40% chance of causing [Small Electric Shock]

Seer ​

  • Blade Changer:
    • Cool time: 8s -> 11s
  • [Armour of Darkness]
    • Non-elemental damage received is decreased by 25% -> 20%
    • Heal 35% -> 30% of inflicted damage by reducing MP

Others​

Zenas' Divine Ring ​

  • [Goddess' Favour]:
    • Up to level 99 there is a 100% -> 30% chance of never getting a bad effect.
