Introduction

Wings without Additional Effect Effect
Archangel Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Reduces the enemy's light resistance by 10.
- Light element is increased by 100.
Archdaemon Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Reduces the enemy's shadow resistance by 10.
- Shadow element is increased by 100.
Blazing Fire Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Reduces the enemy's fire resistance by 10.
- Fire element is increased by 100.
Frosty Ice Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Reduces the enemy's water resistance by 10.
- Water element is increased by 100.
Golden Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Resists forced movement with a probability of 50%.
- Damage received is decreased by 10%.
Titan Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Increases PvP attack power by 10%.
- Defence power in PvP is increased by 5%.
Fairy Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- The equipped fairy's element increases by 15.
- Provides a 5% chance to reset the cooldown of the attack skill used.
Crystal Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Up to level 4 there is a 15% chance of never getting a bad effect.
- Every 4 seconds: Restores (Player Level*3) HP.
Wings with Additional Effect EffectAdditional Buff/Debuffs
Onyx Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- When attacking, there is a 20% chance of summoning a shadow clone to carry out an additional attack.
- Damage dealt in PvE is increased by 5%. 		The attack of the Shadow clone will apply 25% as true damage of the attack that was being carried out.
Mega Titan Wings- Damage received in PvP is reduced by 7%.
- Damage dealt in PvP is increased by 7%.

- Titan Transformation occurs in releasing at the probability of 100% every 30 seconds

Mega Titan Booster. If the SP card has been upgraded to +15, there is a chance of the Titan Transformation being activated.

[Titan Transformation]

Duration: 5 seconds

- Never receives critical hits.
- Movement speed is increased by 2.
Zephyr Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- Movement speed is increased by 10%.

- Western Wind occurs in releasing at the probability of 100% every 30 seconds

 [Western Wind]

Duration: 2 seconds

- Movement speed is increased by 3.
- All dodges are increased by (Player Level*4).
- A shadowy figure appears.
Lightning Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- On attack, there is a 5% chance of inflicting Electrocute on your opponent.
- When you're defending, there is a 7% chance of receiving Static Charge.		 [Electrocute]

Duration: 10 seconds

- Every 2 seconds: HP is reduced by (Player Level*4).
- Move in a different direction against the player's will for 5 seconds.

[Static Charge]

- Gather 3000 points for the next attack.
Blade Wings- Movement speed is increased by 1.
- When you're defending, there is a 10% chance of receiving Shield of Blades.
- Every 2 seconds: At a distance of 3, inflict Wounded on enemies.		 [Shield of Blades]

Duration: 5 seconds

- Enemy's HP is decreased by 20% of the damage I have received.
- Damage dealt is increased by 10%.

[Wounded]

Duration: 8 seconds

- All attacks are decreased by (Player Levelx3).
- Every 2 seconds: HP is reduced by (Player Levelx3).
